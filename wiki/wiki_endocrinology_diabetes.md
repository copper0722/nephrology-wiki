# Endocrinology Diabetes — Journal Reading Wiki

## Oral Semaglutide in High-Risk Type 2 Diabetes (2025)
DOI: 10.1056/NEJMoa2501006
Design: RCT, double-blind, placebo-controlled, event-driven superiority
Key: Oral semaglutide reduced MACE (HR 0.86, 95% CI 0.77–0.96) in T2DM with ASCVD/CKD; no safety signal.
Clinical: Establishes CV efficacy of oral GLP-1RA in high-risk populations; supports clinical adoption.
Numbers: MACE 12.0% vs 13.8%, N=9650, median follow-up 49.5 mo

## Senotherapeutics in Diabetic Kidney Disease (2025)
DOI: 10.1681/ASN.0000000754
Design: Narrative review (JASN Perspective)
Key: Cellular senescence and inflammation drive DKD; senolytics/senomorphics + regenerative medicine offer complementary mechanisms beyond SGLT2i/GLP-1RA.
Clinical: Emerging multi-target approach for refractory DKD; addresses unmet need in non-responders.
Numbers: N/A

## Tirzepatide vs Semaglutide for Obesity (2025)
DOI: 10.1056/NEJMoa2416394
Design: RCT, phase 3b, open-label, head-to-head
Key: Tirzepatide (GIP/GLP-1 agonist) superior to semaglutide for weight loss: -20.2% vs -13.7% (P<0.001).
Clinical: First direct comparison; dual agonism more effective; supports tirzepatide for obesity monotherapy.
Numbers: N=751, 72 weeks; waist circumference -18.4cm vs -13.0cm (P<0.001)

## Decade of Progress: SGLT2i & GLP-1RA in T2DM & CVD (2025)
DOI: 10.3389/fendo.2025.1605746
Design: Narrative review
Key: SGLT2i reduce HF hospitalization; GLP-1RA reduce atherothrombotic events; combination therapy complementary with additive cardiorenal benefits.
Clinical: Guideline-supported dual therapy paradigm; risk-stratified selection based on phenotype.
Numbers: N/A

## Orforglipron, Oral GLP-1RA for Obesity (2025)
DOI: 10.1056/NEJMoa2511774
Design: RCT, phase 3, once-daily oral
Key: Non-peptide oral GLP-1RA achieves -11.2% weight loss at 36mg dose vs placebo -2.1% (P<0.001); 54.6% ≥10% loss.
Clinical: Advances oral GLP-1RA class; improves adherence vs injectables; favorable tolerability profile.
Numbers: N=3127, 72 weeks; dose-dependent response (6mg -7.5%, 12mg -8.4%, 36mg -11.2%)

Clinical: Systemic pleiotropy supports use even in non-diabetic CKD/HF; mechanism-driven expansion of indications.

Clinical: CGM accurate tool for glycemic monitoring; AID needs phase 3 evidence in dialysis population before adoption.

## Metformin for Knee Osteoarthritis in Overweight/Obese (2025)
DOI: 10.1001/jama.2025.3471
Design: RCT, double-blind, placebo-controlled
Key: Metformin 2000mg/day significantly reduces knee pain (VAS -31.3mm vs -18.9mm, P<0.001) via AMPK-mediated anti-inflammatory mechanism.
Clinical: Repurposing common antidiabetic for OA; addresses metabolic drivers of cartilage degeneration.
Numbers: N=~200, 6 months; WOMAC scores improved significantly in pain/stiffness/function domains

Design: Editorial commentary
Key: AID systems promise to transform T2DM insulin therapy but face major barriers: cost, provider training, diabetes educator shortage, software recall risk.
Clinical: Technology proven effective but implementation requires healthcare system restructuring and workforce expansion.
Numbers: N/A

## Randomized Trial of AID in Type 2 Diabetes (2025)
DOI: 10.1056/NEJMoa2415948
Design: RCT, multicenter, randomized, controlled (2:1 AID vs standard insulin)
Key: Control-IQ+ AID improves HbA1c (8.2%→7.3%, Δ-0.9%) and increases time-in-range; daily insulin reduced 8 units.
Clinical: Largest RCT demonstrating AID efficacy in insulin-treated T2DM; supports expanded access; weight gain +2.4kg concern.
Numbers: N=319, 13 weeks; HbA1c Δ-1.6% in subgroup HbA1c≥9% at baseline

## Paget's Disease of Bone: Case Report (2025)
DOI: 10.1056/NEJMicm2414362
Design: Case report
Key: 80-year-old female with progressive frontal bossing and sensorineural hearing loss; histologic hallmark: cotton-wool appearance of skull.
Clinical: Rare metabolic bone disorder; late diagnosis from missed imaging 16 years prior; complications include deafness, fracture risk.
Numbers: Alkaline phosphatase 592 IU/L (normal 38–113); radionuclide bone scan shows increased uptake in skull/pelvis
